1. Introduction

Welcome to Moro! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our productivity application. By using Moro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data Collected

Personal Information

  • Name and Email Address: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address for authentication and personalization
  • Authentication Data: We use Firebase Authentication to securely manage your login credentials
  • User Profile: Your display name and account preferences stored in Firestore

Task and Content Data

  • Task Content: The tasks, reminders, and notes you create in the app
  • Task Metadata: Completion status, creation dates, scheduling information, and time settings
  • Calendar Data: Your selected dates, task organization, and scheduling preferences
  • Productivity Data: Monthly productivity scores and task completion statistics for widgets

Local Device Data

  • Widget Data: Productivity scores and task statistics cached locally for widget functionality
  • Notification Data: Local notification settings and scheduled reminders
  • App Preferences: User interface preferences and settings stored locally

Firebase Services Data Collection

Firebase Authentication

  • User Credentials: Email addresses and authentication tokens
  • Account Information: User IDs, creation timestamps, and login history
  • Security Data: Authentication state and session management

Cloud Firestore

  • User Documents: Name, email, and account creation date
  • Task Collections: All your tasks, reminders, and associated metadata
  • User Preferences: App settings and customization data

Firebase Analytics (Disabled)

  • Note: Firebase Analytics is currently disabled in our app configuration
  • No Analytics Data: We do not collect analytics or usage tracking data
  • No User Behavior Tracking: We do not track how you use the app

Firebase Crashlytics (Not Enabled)

  • Note: Firebase Crashlytics is not enabled in our app
  • No Crash Reporting: We do not collect crash reports or error logs
  • No Performance Data: We do not collect app performance metrics
  • No Device Analytics: We do not collect device specifications for debugging

3. Purpose of Collection

Core App Functionality

  • Task Management: Store, sync, and manage your tasks across devices
  • User Authentication: Secure login and account management
  • Data Synchronization: Keep your data consistent across all your devices
  • Local Notifications: Send you reminders for scheduled tasks and important updates
  • Widget Functionality: Display productivity statistics on your device's home screen
  • Calendar Integration: Organize tasks by date and provide calendar views
  • Productivity Tracking: Calculate and display monthly productivity scores

Technical Operations

  • Security: Protect your account and data from unauthorized access through Firebase Authentication
  • Service Reliability: Ensure consistent service availability and performance through Firebase infrastructure
  • Data Protection: Ensure your data is safely stored and recoverable through Firebase's infrastructure and redundancy systems

User Experience

  • Personalization: Customize the app experience based on your preferences
  • Account Management: Maintain your user profile and app settings
  • Task History: Provide historical view of your tasks with filtering options
  • Monthly Analytics: Show productivity trends and task completion statistics

5. Data Security and Storage

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest through Firebase's security infrastructure
  • Firebase Security: Google's enterprise-grade security infrastructure
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular Updates: Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored securely in Firebase's encrypted cloud database
  • Local Storage: Temporary local storage for app functionality and caching
  • Backup Systems: Firebase's infrastructure redundancy and data protection systems to prevent data loss
  • Data Retention: Data retained only as long as necessary for service provision

6. User Rights

Access Rights

  • View Your Data: Access all your tasks, account information, and app data through the app
  • Account Information: View and update your profile information in Account Settings
  • Task History: View all your tasks with filtering options (All Time, This Week, This Month, Last Month)
  • Productivity Data: View your monthly productivity scores and task statistics

Control Rights

  • Update Information: Modify your name in Account Settings
  • Change Password: Update your account password through the app
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Data Correction: Correct any inaccurate personal information
  • Notification Control: Manage local notification settings through your device

Deletion Rights

  • Account Deletion: Complete account deletion removes all your data from Firestore
  • Data Deletion: All personal data permanently deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • Local Data: All local data (widgets, notifications, cached data) cleared upon account deletion
  • Firebase Data: All data removed from Firebase services immediately

Contact for Rights

7. Data Retention

Account Data

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: All data permanently deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • No Data Retention: We do not retain any data after account deletion

Task Data

  • Synced Tasks: Stored in Firebase's encrypted cloud database until deleted by user
  • Local Tasks: Stored on your device until synced or deleted
  • Widget Data: Cached locally for widget functionality, cleared when account is deleted

Local Data

  • Notifications: Stored locally on your device, cleared when account is deleted
  • Widget Cache: Productivity data cached for widgets, cleared when account is deleted
  • App Preferences: Stored locally, cleared when account is deleted
